About Barua's Sandwich Corner

Barua's Sandwich Corner provides fresh and delicious sandwiches in a welcoming fast-casual environment. We serve house-made, hand-crafted sandwiches for customers who want quality food and friendly service.

Our restaurant is a great spot for dine-in, take-out, catering, private events, parties, and meetings. We are located on Gladstone Avenue in Ottawa and are proud to serve our local community.

Barua's Sandwich Corner was founded in 2001 by Barua J. Since then, we have focused on providing delicious food, reliable service, and a comfortable experience for every customer.
