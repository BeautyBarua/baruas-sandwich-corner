Our Staff

Meet the people behind Barua's Sandwich Corner.

Our Team

Our team is dedicated to providing delicious food, friendly service, and a welcoming restaurant experience for every customer.

Greg Amyotte

Owner

Greg Amyotte is the owner of Barua's Sandwich Corner. He brings restaurant experience, leadership, and a strong focus on customer service to the business. His goal is to create a welcoming place where customers can enjoy fresh and delicious sandwiches.

Jayson Brunet

Managing Partner

Jayson Brunet is the managing partner of Barua's Sandwich Corner. He has experience in the food industry and helps manage the daily restaurant operations. He enjoys working with both customers and staff to create a positive restaurant experience.

Phil Thibeault

Chef

Chef Phil prepares food with care and creativity. His cooking style focuses on quality ingredients, fresh flavours, and a welcoming dining experience. He helps make sure every sandwich is prepared with attention and care.

Marc Andre

General Manager

Marc Andre is the general manager of Barua's Sandwich Corner. He supports the team, helps manage restaurant service, and brings a positive attitude to the workplace. His leadership helps keep the restaurant organized and customer-focused.
